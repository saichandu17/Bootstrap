The Director serves under the Board of Governors and is the school's chief academic and executive officer. Academic policies are decided by its Senate, which is composed of some professors and other representatives. The Senate controls and approves the curriculum, courses, examinations, and results. Senate committees examine specific academic matters.

NIT Durgapur has been ranked at 96th position for its overall performance by NIRF Rankings in the year 2020. The institute has also excelled in its academic curriculum for which it has been ranked at 47th position in top engineering colleges by NIRF Rankings 2020. Its flagship program is B.Tech with CSE specialization.

The institute is fully residential.Taking accommodation is compulsory for every student.There are seven hostels for boys and two hostels for girls. A boys hostel is under construction as also are new wings in three of the existing boys hostels. A canteen for the students and the staff is located near the main academic building and there is a second canteen at the rear exit of the main academic building of the Institute. A cafeteria is situated near the boys hostels.
The institute awards Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) degrees in nine engineering disciplines. As of 2019, the annual intake for the B.Tech. programs were over 1000, with a student-faculty ratio of 25:1. The B.Tech. and B.Arch. degree programs are four and five years long respectively. Courses are allotted a number of credits according to their importance. Each academic year is divided into two semesters and teaching programs are organized around the credit system. Teaching includes lectures, tutorials, practicals, projects, seminars, dissertations, and field and industrial training
The annual Techno-management festival of National Institute of Technology Durgapur, Aarohan is usually held in the month of February every year. It comprises various events like competitions, exhibits, workshops and talks from guest speakers. Aarohan 2021 was conducted in virtual mode.
The NIT Durgapur's Department of Management Studies (DoMS) offers a Master of Business Administration program. They offered Dual specialization in Marketing, Finance, Business Analytics, and IT Consulting, Human Resources, and Operations. DoMS Curriculum is designed with inputs from industry experts, faculty from IIM's, and other reputed B-Schools.
